About Me

Linus Kipkoech is currently a third year Electrical Engineering and Computer Science student at the University of California Berkeley. In May 2018, he will graduate with a Bachelor of Science. He is a member of a number of professional and student organizations, including the Black Engineering and Science Association, UC Berkeley Computer Science Scholars, and Computer Science Undergraduate Association. During the last two years, Linus has written more than five thousand lines of code in Java, Python, C, and MIPs Assembly language. He is good at data structures and algorithms. Moreover, Linus has taken courses in designing information devices and systems, and integrated circuit device fabrication and surface micro matching technology. These experiences have provided him with valuable experience and insight in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering.

I am interested in cyber security and Micro fabrication Technilogy.
